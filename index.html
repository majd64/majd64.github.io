About Fresh Auto Detailing

10% of our revnue will go to charity

Fresh Auto Detailing provides professional Automotive Mobile Detailing services. We believe in treating our customers like family and providing them with the best results they deserve. We operate within the Windsor Essex region, including LaSalle, Lakeshore, and Tecumseh. If you have a busy schedule and can't bring your vehicle to a professional shop, don't worry, because our professional shop will be at your own driveway. We set up our mobile tent onsite to replicate the controlled shop environment and use the best products to give you the best experience and ensure your satisfaction.
